1. Purpose & Engagement

This Agreement outlines the collaboration between Songtrybe and the A&R Representative for talent scouting, artist development, and music acquisition. Company hereby engages A&R Representative to provide artists and repertoire services as described herein. A&R Representative accepts such engagement and agrees to provide such services in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

2. Services Provided by A&R Representative

Detailed in Section 3 above - includes Talent Scouting, Artist Evaluation, Industry Networking, Live Performance Scouting, Artist Development, Creative Direction, Recording Oversight, and Reporting & Analysis.

3. Term

The initial term of this Agreement shall be for a period of one (1) year commencing on the date of execution. This Agreement may be renewed for additional periods upon mutual written agreement of the parties.

4. Compensation

In consideration for the services provided, A&R Representative shall receive:

  • A monthly retainer as specified in Section 4 above, payable on the first day of each month;
  • A finder's fee equal to the percentage specified in Section 4 above of the initial advance paid to any artist signed to the Company as a direct result of A&R Representative's efforts;
  • A commission on net profits generated by artists signed to the Company as a direct result of A&R Representative's efforts, for the period specified in Section 4 above following the signing of such artist.
