3. Complete Contract Terms & Conditions ▼
Please review all 9 sections of the dancer contract below:
1. Purpose

This Agreement outlines the collaboration between Songtrybe and the professional dancer for performance bookings, marketing, and entertainment industry visibility. It provides mutual value through exposure, performance opportunities, and co-branded promotional activities within the dance and entertainment industry.

2. Services Provided by Songtrybe

Detailed in Section 2 above - includes Dancer Profile Showcase, Entertainment Industry Marketing, Performance Opportunities, Dancer Network Access, Brand Collaborations, Performance Analytics, Professional Development, and Video Portfolio Support.

3. Use of Name, Likeness, and Content

The Dancer grants Songtrybe a royalty-free, worldwide, non-exclusive right to:

  • Display the Dancer's name, image, dance styles, performance videos, and professional branding on Songtrybe's platforms.
  • Use the Dancer's likeness and performance footage in marketing materials targeting entertainment industry professionals.
  • Feature submitted content (dance reels, performance videos, photos) across Songtrybe's digital channels and promotional campaigns.
  • Include Dancer's credentials, testimonials, and performance history in industry marketing materials.
  • Create promotional content featuring the Dancer's performances for platform marketing purposes.
4. Referral & Incentive Program

Dancer Referrals: Dancers who refer other performers, choreographers, venues, or entertainment professionals that join Songtrybe may receive bonuses (e.g., $60 per successful referral or priority booking status).

Featured Artist Opportunities: Top-performing dancers may be featured in Songtrybe promotional campaigns, dance showcases, or branded entertainment content.

Performance Incentives: Dancers with exceptional client feedback and booking success may receive enhanced promotional support and premium profile features.

Collaboration Bonuses: Additional compensation opportunities for dancers participating in Songtrybe's branded events or promotional videos.

5. Expectations & Responsibilities
  • Dancers agree to maintain current and accurate profiles including availability, dance styles, and portfolio content.
  • Songtrybe agrees to use reasonable efforts to promote listed dancers but does not guarantee any specific number of bookings or performance opportunities.
  • Dancers must maintain professional performance standards including punctuality, appropriate attire, rehearsal attendance, and adherence to choreography requirements.
  • Dancers agree to provide high-quality performances and maintain positive relationships with clients, venues, and fellow performers.
  • Dancers must respect venue policies, safety requirements, and performance guidelines at all booked events.
6. Ownership & Rights

Dancer retains all ownership rights to their original choreography, performance concepts, and personal branding materials.

Songtrybe has limited promotional rights for submitted performance videos and dance content for the term of this Agreement.

Performance recordings created during Songtrybe-facilitated events may be used by both parties for promotional purposes.

Client relationships developed through Songtrybe referrals remain accessible to both parties for future collaboration opportunities.

7. Term & Termination

This Agreement is effective upon signature and remains active unless terminated by either party with 14 days' written notice.

Upon termination: Songtrybe will remove the Dancer's profile and stop using their content in new marketing campaigns. Previously published performance content may remain for historical or platform integrity purposes.

Confirmed performances: Any booked performances at time of termination will be honored by both parties to maintain professional commitments.

Portfolio content: Dancer may request removal of specific performance videos or promotional materials within 30 days of termination.

8. Non-Circumvention

Dancer agrees not to bypass Songtrybe in any direct deals resulting from Songtrybe-originated client introductions, casting opportunities, or performance referrals without prior written consent.

For any direct bookings resulting from Songtrybe referrals, Dancer agrees to notify Songtrybe and optionally share a 15% commission if the performance booking was facilitated through the platform.

Pre-existing relationships: Clients and venues with established relationships prior to joining Songtrybe are exempt from this clause.

Collaborative projects: Multi-dancer performances arranged through Songtrybe are subject to platform coordination requirements.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [Insert State].

Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through binding arbitration in the selected state, with consideration for the unique nature of performance contracts and entertainment industry standards.
