1. Purpose

This Agreement outlines the collaboration between Songtrybe and the Master of Ceremony (MC) for event hosting, marketing, and platform visibility. It provides mutual value through exposure, hosting opportunities, and co-branded promotional activities.

2. Services Provided by Songtrybe

Detailed in Section 2 above - includes MC Profile Listing, Targeted Marketing, Booking Opportunities, Event Host Network, Brand Partnerships, Performance Analytics, and Professional Development resources.

3. Use of Name, Likeness, and Content

The MC grants Songtrybe a royalty-free, worldwide, non-exclusive right to:

  • Display the MC's name, image, biography, hosting specialties, and branding on Songtrybe's platforms.
  • Use the MC's likeness in marketing materials, event promotions, and press campaigns targeting event planners.
  • Feature any submitted content (e.g., hosting demos, event videos, testimonials) on Songtrybe's digital channels.
  • Include MC's professional credentials and client testimonials in promotional materials.
4. Referral & Incentive Program

MC Referrals: MCs who refer event venues, other hosts, or entertainment professionals that join Songtrybe may receive bonuses (e.g., $75 per successful referral or priority booking status).

Featured Host Opportunities: Top-performing MCs may be featured in Songtrybe editorial content, hosting showcases, or branded event campaigns.

Performance Bonuses: MCs with exceptional client feedback may receive additional promotional support and premium listing features.

5. Expectations & Responsibilities
  • MCs agree to keep their profile, availability, and specialties current and accurate.
  • Songtrybe agrees to use reasonable efforts to promote listed MCs but does not guarantee any specific number of bookings or events.
  • MCs must maintain professional hosting standards when representing Songtrybe, including punctuality, appropriate attire, engaging presentation, and adherence to event requirements.
  • MCs agree to provide professional-quality hosting services and maintain positive client relationships.
6. Ownership & Rights

MC retains all ownership rights to their content, scripts, and professional branding materials.

Songtrybe has limited promotional rights for submitted hosting materials and professional content for the term of this Agreement.

Client relationships developed through Songtrybe referrals remain the property of both parties for future collaboration.

7. Term & Termination

This Agreement is effective upon signature and remains active unless terminated by either party with 14 days' written notice.

Upon termination: Songtrybe will remove the MC's profile and stop using their content in new marketing campaigns. Previously published content may remain for historical or platform integrity purposes.

Ongoing events: Any confirmed bookings at time of termination will be honored by both parties.

8. Non-Circumvention

MC agrees not to bypass Songtrybe in any direct deals resulting from Songtrybe-originated client introductions or event referrals without prior written consent.

For any direct bookings resulting from Songtrybe referrals, MC agrees to notify Songtrybe and optionally share a 12% commission if the booking was facilitated through the platform.

Exception: Pre-existing client relationships established before joining Songtrybe are exempt from this clause.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [Insert State].

Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through binding arbitration in the selected state.
